About

HearWear

HearWear is an innovative project designed to revolutionize
communication for individuals with partial hearing loss. It
functions both as a hearing aid and a real-time language
translator, all operated seamlessly through in-ear devices
such as earbuds or earphones. By enhancing ambient sounds and
converting speech into amplified, clearer audio, HearWear aids
those with partial deafness in better understanding their
surroundings. Simultaneously, its built-in live translation
feature allows users to break language barriers, making
conversations across different languages more accessible.
